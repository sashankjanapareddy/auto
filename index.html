About Our Museum

Founded in 1985, the Vintage Lens Museum is dedicated to preserving the history and artistry of photography through its most important tool: the camera.

Our collection features over 200 cameras from the late 19th century to the 1970s, tracing the evolution of photographic technology and its impact on visual storytelling.

Each camera in our collection has been meticulously restored to functional condition, with many still capable of capturing images today just as they did decades ago.
